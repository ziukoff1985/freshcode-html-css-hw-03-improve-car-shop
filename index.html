FORD

History

The Ford Motor Company was founded by Henry Ford on June 16, 1903. Its goal was to make the automobile accessible to the general public, not just the wealthy. This ambitious idea was brought to life through the implementation of assembly line production, which significantly reduced the cost of assembling cars. The legendary Ford Model T, released in 1908, became a symbol of mass motorization. It was so popular that over 15 million units were sold by 1927. Over the years, Ford continued to innovate, expanding its model range and introducing new technologies, allowing the brand to remain one of the leaders in the global automotive industry.

Best Models

Ford F-150

The Ford F-150 is a full-size pickup truck that has led the US market in sales for decades. Known for its exceptional strength, reliability, and versatility, the F-150 offers a wide range of configurations, engines (including hybrid options), and trim levels. It is ideal for both heavy-duty work and everyday use, offering a spacious cabin and comfortable driving.

Ford Focus

The Ford Focus is a compact passenger car that gained popularity due to its dynamic design, handling, and fuel efficiency. The Focus offers a balanced combination of comfort and practicality, making it an excellent choice for city driving and long journeys. Available in hatchback and wagon body styles, it is one of Ford's bestsellers in Europe.

TOYOTA

History

The history of Toyota Motor Corporation began in 1937 when Kiichiro Toyoda founded the automotive company as a spin-off from Toyota Industries, which produced weaving looms. Toyota's main philosophy has always been the pursuit of excellence and continuous improvement, known as "Kaizen." The company became a global leader thanks to its innovative production systems, which ensured high quality, efficiency, and reliability. It is thanks to this that Toyota has earned the trust of millions of consumers worldwide, offering cars known for their durability and low operating costs.

Best Models

Toyota Camry

The Toyota Camry is a mid-size sedan that has been one of the best-selling cars in its class for many years. The Camry has earned a reputation for its exceptional reliability, comfortable cabin, smooth ride, and excellent fuel economy (especially in the hybrid version). It offers a spacious interior, modern safety technologies, and infotainment systems, making it an ideal choice for both family use and business trips.

Toyota RAV4

The Toyota RAV4 is a compact crossover that was one of the pioneers in this market segment. The RAV4 combines the comfort of a passenger car with the practicality and off-road capability of an SUV. It is known for its reliability, fuel efficiency (hybrid versions are also available), spacious interior, and significant ground clearance. The RAV4 is great for both urban conditions and out-of-town trips, offering a modern design and a wide range of features.

BMW

History

Bayerische Motoren Werke AG (BMW) was founded in 1916 in Munich, Germany. Initially, the company was engaged in the production of aircraft engines, hence the distinctive logo symbolizing a rotating propeller against a blue sky. After World War I, BMW switched to manufacturing motorcycles, and the first car, the BMW 3/15 Dixi, was released in 1928. Since then, BMW has gained worldwide recognition due to its focus on sportiness, luxury, innovation, and engineering excellence. BMW cars are known for their powerful engines, excellent handling, and premium quality, making them desirable for lovers of dynamic driving.

Best Models

BMW 3 Series

The BMW 3 Series is BMW's most popular model, serving as a benchmark in the premium compact sports sedan segment. It offers an ideal combination of dynamics, comfort, and style. The 3 Series is famous for its excellent driving performance, precise steering, powerful and fuel-efficient engines, and exquisite interior. It is a car for those who value driving pleasure without compromising on everyday practicality.

BMW X5

The BMW X5 is a mid-size premium SUV that combines luxury, performance, and versatility. The X5 was one of the first Sports Activity Vehicles (SAV) to offer a combination of BMW dynamics with SUV functionality. It features a spacious and luxurious interior, advanced technologies, powerful engines, and impressive handling, despite its size. The X5 is ideal for families looking for comfort, safety, and prestige.
